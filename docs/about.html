HTML5 Video Every Where

Replace video player with browser native video player.

Some streaming sites video players suck. This add-on will replace them with the browser native video player. Currently, it supports YouTube, Facebook, Vimeo, Dailymotion, Break, Metacafe and even more sites are coming...

Thanks for browsers and Linux distributions developers who chosed to provide this extension built-into their products (including GNU IceCat, Trisquel).

Thanks for your donations and for supporting us.
